1. If you haven't closed the audio panel, so so now. Now select the Settings menu (the gear) and tap on Fix All Measure Duration Totals. This will either truncate or add rests to measures whose total duration is less than or exceeds the time signature.

Discussion: If you plan to import your score into a desktop music notation application such as Finale, it is advisable to make sure all of your measures have legal durations.

2. Under the share icon tap on Export In Email... then tap on MusicXML. This will bring up an email client. Fill out the recipient and any other text you would like to add and tap on Send.

Discussion: There are a variety of export formats to attach in an email and two formats (MusicXML and MIDI) that can also be saved to your iPad. When you choose Export MusicXML or Export MIDI (the first two options in the share menu), you will be able to find your files when you next synchronize with iTunes.

Result: You have learned how to export.
