Making selections.
4

1. There are several ways to select notes and other events in your score.

2. Tap on anything - clef, meter, note, tempo or text marking, etc. Then tap somewhere in the clear (outside of a staff) to deselect everything.

3. Tap on the pencil to put MusicJOT in handwriting mode. Draw a complete circle around a note or notes. Make sure the circle is both closed and extends above and below the staff. Exit handwriting mode.

4. In palette mode (handwriting is off) double tap and drag making sure to start outside of the staff and in the clear. This will drag out a selection marquee.

5. In palette mode double tap in a measure but not on any notes or events. This will select a whole measure. You can continue to select other measures by dragging.

Discussion: After selecting an item or items, you will also see a context sensitive menu appear at the bottom of the screen. This will show you various functions you can perform on the selection.

Result: You have learned how to make selections in both palette and handwriting mode.
